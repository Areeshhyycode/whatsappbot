WHAT HAPPENS AFTER YOU APPLY
----------------------------
- Within 5 working days, our HR team reviews your application.
- If shortlisted, we send an email with the next step.
- If not shortlisted, we still send a reply so you are not left waiting.


OUR INTERVIEW PROCESS (TYPICAL)
-------------------------------
Stage 1 — HR Screening (20 minutes, phone or WhatsApp call)
  We get to know you, your background, and answer your questions.

Stage 2 — Technical / Skill Round (45–60 minutes, online)
  Role-specific. For engineers, a coding problem or take-home task.
  For designers, a portfolio walkthrough. For marketing, a brief task.

Stage 3 — Team Interview (45 minutes, online or onsite)
  Meet your potential teammates and your manager.

Stage 4 — Final Round with Leadership (30 minutes)
  Discuss your goals, ours, and check mutual fit.

Total time end-to-end: usually 2 to 3 weeks.

COMMON CANDIDATE QUESTIONS
--------------------------

Q: I applied last week. When will I hear back?
A: HR replies within 5 working days. If it has been longer, email
   careers@[yourcompany].com with your name and the role.

Q: Can I apply for more than one role?
A: Yes — but apply separately for each role, with a note on why each fits.

Q: Do you sponsor visas or relocation?
A: For senior engineering roles only, decided case by case.

Q: What is the dress code for the interview?
A: Business casual. No need for a suit. Be comfortable.

Q: Can the interview be over Zoom or Google Meet?
A: Yes — all early-stage interviews are online. We use Google Meet.

Q: I have a 1-month notice period. Is that okay?
A: Yes, that is normal. Please mention it in your interview so we can plan.

Q: Are there any internships available?
A: Yes, we hire 2–3 interns per year in HR and Engineering. See the open
   positions list above.

Q: I am a fresher with no experience. Can I apply?
A: Yes — for our intern and entry-level roles. Mention projects, courses or
   open-source contributions to stand out.

Q: How do I prepare for the technical round?
A: Brush up on the basics for the role. We care more about how you think
   than perfect syntax. For coding rounds, no need to memorize algorithms.

Q: I missed my scheduled interview. What now?
A: Email careers@[yourcompany].com as soon as possible to reschedule.
   We usually offer one reschedule.

Q: Can I get feedback if I am rejected?
A: After a final-round rejection, yes — we send a short note with one or two
   points to help your next attempts.

Q: Where is the office?
A: [Full Address]. The nearest landmark is [Landmark].

Q: Do you hire part-time?
A: Only for design and marketing on a contract basis, occasionally.

Q: Will I be paid for the take-home task?
A: Take-home tasks are short (under 4 hours) and unpaid; longer paid tasks
   are sometimes offered for senior roles.
